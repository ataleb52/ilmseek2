Support an Ilm Seeker

Ilm Seekers aren't just another kid in your community. They're the ones with the ideas, innovativeness, and will power to push our community forward.


Supporting an Ilm Seeker can be done in two ways.

  1. Sponsoring an Ilm Seeker
    • Sponsoring an Ilm Seeker not only assists them in covering their fees, but it shows support from the community that they need to succeed. It's also a way of creating a source of sadakah jariah or ongoing charity for yourself, long after you've sponsored them. These Ilm Seekers will be bringing forth change, creating new projects and programs that benefit others and those less fortunate. These actions will be an an investment of an ongoing source of good deeds for not only the Ilm Seeker, but for yourself.

    Sponsor an Ilm Seeker

    (Please write "Ilm Seeker" in receipt instructions)
  2. Mentoring an Ilm Seeker
    • As Ilm Seekers work on their projects, they'll be in need of mentorship to help guide them. Mentoring an Ilm Seeker can be as simple as looking over their idea, or as involved as getting down in the trenches with helping make their idea happen. Whatever you choose, your experiences, feedback, and input will be a great asset to them. Not only that, but just like a sponsor, you too will have a hand in the sadakah jariah from these Ilm Seekers.

    Mentor an Ilm Seeker

    (Or you can email youth@ictucson.org)
